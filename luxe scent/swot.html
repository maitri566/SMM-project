Strategic Analysis

Digital Marketing & Brand Positioning for Luxe Scents

Strengths

  • Premium Brand Identity: High-end visual aesthetic and artisanal focus differentiate us from mass-market retailers.
  • Immersive User Experience: Integration of high-quality video backgrounds and detailed olfactory descriptions.
  • Niche Product Selection: Exclusive focus on rare notes targeting high-intent luxury demographics.

Weaknesses

  • High Entry Price Point: Premium pricing (₹2,499+) may limit market share among certain consumers.
  • Limited Physical Presence: Digital-first library means customers cannot sample scents physically (Blind Buy risk).
  • New Brand Authority: Lower organic search ranking compared to established giants in the "Luxury Perfume" space.

Opportunities

  • Scent Discovery Kits: Launching sample-sized discovery sets to lower barriers for new customers.
  • Influencer Collaborations: Partnering with "Perfume-tok" creators to showcase the "Scent Story".
  • AI Scent Profiling: Implementing an AI quiz to help users find their "Signature Fragrance".

Threats

  • Counterfeit Markets: The rise of "dupe" fragrances at a fraction of the cost targeting our audience.
  • Rising Ingredient Costs: Supply chain volatility for rare ingredients affecting margins.
  • Platform Dependency: High reliance on social media algorithms for luxury traffic acquisition.
